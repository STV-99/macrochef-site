Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 2026

MacroChef is a cooking and nutrition app. This policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and what we never do with it.

What we collect

Email address
When you sign in, we ask for your email address to send you a sign-in link. We store it securely and use it only to authenticate your account.

Usage data
We collect anonymous events about how you use the app — for example, when you import a recipe, log a meal, or use the nutrition tracker. This helps us understand which features are working well and which need improvement. Events contain no personal details beyond your user ID.

Recipe import URLs
When you import a recipe from a website or video link (YouTube, TikTok, Instagram, etc.), we process that URL on our servers to extract recipe content. We log these requests to monitor quality and debug failures. We do not store the full content of pages you import indefinitely.

What we do NOT collect

  • We do not collect your name, phone number, or address.
  • We do not access your contacts, camera, microphone, or location.
  • We do not track you across other apps or websites.
  • We do not use third-party advertising SDKs.

How we use your information

  • To authenticate your account via email sign-in
  • To save your recipes, meal plans, and nutrition goals across devices
  • To improve app features based on anonymous usage patterns
  • To diagnose and fix errors in recipe import processing

We do not sell your data

We do not sell, rent, or share your personal information with third parties for marketing or advertising purposes. Full stop.

Third-party services

MacroChef uses the following third-party services to operate:

  • Supabase — authentication and cloud data storage
  • Groq / OpenRouter — AI processing for recipe extraction and chef responses
  • USDA FoodData Central & Open Food Facts — publicly available nutrition data

Each of these services has its own privacy policy. We only share the minimum data necessary for them to function.

Data retention

Your account data (recipes, meal plans, nutrition goals) is stored as long as your account exists. You can request deletion of your account and all associated data at any time by contacting us.

Children's privacy

MacroChef is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from anyone under 13.

Changes to this policy

If we make material changes to this policy, we will update the date at the top of this page. Continued use of the app after changes means you accept the updated policy.

Contact

Questions or requests? Email us at support@macrochef.app.
